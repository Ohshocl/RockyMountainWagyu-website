Whole Beef

The complete Rocky Mountain Wagyu experience

What You Get

A whole beef provides approximately 400-600 pounds of packaged beef, depending on the animal. You'll receive a complete variety of cuts including premium steaks, roasts, ground beef, and specialty cuts.

Premium Steaks

Ribeyes, NY strips, filet mignon, sirloin, and T-bones with exceptional marbling

Roasts & Brisket

Chuck roasts, rump roasts, brisket perfect for slow cooking

Ground Beef

High-quality ground beef in convenient 1-2 lb packages

Specialty Cuts

Short ribs, stew meat, and other specialty options

Investment & Value

Whole beef represents the best value per pound and provides maximum variety. Contact us for current pricing and availability.

Perfect For

Large families, multiple households sharing, or anyone wanting to stock their freezer with a full year's supply of premium Wagyu beef. Requires approximately 16-20 cubic feet of freezer space.

Half Beef

Our most popular choice for families

What You Get

A half beef provides approximately 200-300 pounds of packaged beef. You'll receive a well-balanced selection of all major cuts, perfect for most families.

Balanced Selection

Mix of premium steaks, everyday cuts, and ground beef

Manageable Quantity

Perfect amount for a family of 4-6 for several months

Cut Options

Customize your cutting instructions to match your preferences

Excellent Value

Great price per pound with variety of premium cuts

Investment & Value

Half beef offers excellent value and is our most requested option. Contact us for current pricing and to reserve your half.

Perfect For

Average families looking for premium quality without overwhelming freezer space. Requires approximately 8-10 cubic feet of freezer space.

Quarter Beef

Introduction to premium Wagyu quality

What You Get

A quarter beef provides approximately 100-150 pounds of packaged beef. You'll receive a good variety of cuts, though with less customization options than larger orders.

Variety Pack

Selection of steaks, roasts, and ground beef

Smaller Commitment

Perfect for trying premium Wagyu without large investment

Easy Storage

Fits in standard chest freezer with room to spare

Quality Guaranteed

Same 2.5-year raising process and care

Investment & Value

Quarter beef is a great way to experience Rocky Mountain Wagyu quality. Contact us for current pricing and availability.

Perfect For

Smaller households, first-time bulk beef buyers, or anyone wanting to sample our quality before committing to larger quantities. Requires approximately 4-5 cubic feet of freezer space.

Pre-Sale Program

Reserve your beef from our next generation of calves

How Pre-Sales Work

Our pre-sale program allows you to reserve beef from calves currently being raised. Each calf takes 2.5 years from birth to reach optimal quality and weight for processing.

Program Benefits

Lock In Pricing

Secure today's prices for future delivery

Payment Plans

Track Your Calf

Guaranteed Quality

Same premium raising standards applied

Important Stipulations

Our pre-sale program includes specific terms regarding animal loss, quality variations, refund policies, and weight guarantees. These protections ensure both you and our ranch are treated fairly throughout the 2.5-year raising period.
