Our Approach to Customer Service

At Rocky Mountain Wagyu, we operate on a farming timeline. This means we take the time necessary to provide you with thoughtful, personalized service—the same care and attention we give to raising our Wagyu cattle.

We're not a large commercial operation with instant online ordering. We're a family ranch that believes in building relationships with the families we serve. When you contact us, you're talking directly with the people who raise your beef.

Response Time

We typically respond to inquiries within 24 hours during business days. We may be out on the ranch or in class, but we will get back to you.

Availability

By appointment. We're happy to schedule a time to discuss your needs, answer questions, or arrange a ranch visit.

Orders & Reservations

Contact us to check current availability and place orders. With our 2.5-year raising period, we also offer pre-sales for future calves.

We appreciate your patience and understanding. Quality beef—and quality service—takes time.
