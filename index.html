Why We Raise Wagyu

Our mission is simple: To feed our families healthy, organic beef.

Meet one of our Wagyu calves—each animal receives individual care throughout their 2.5-year journey

We started Rocky Mountain Wagyu because we believe families deserve access to premium, healthy beef raised with integrity. Every decision we make is guided by this simple principle: would we serve this to our own families?

The answer is always yes. From our mountain pastures to your table, we maintain the highest standards of animal welfare, environmental stewardship, and quality control. Our Wagyu cattle are raised without hormones or antibiotics, on natural mountain grazing lands that produce superior marbling and flavor.

This isn't just a business—it's our commitment to you and your family's health and happiness.

What Makes Wagyu Special

Wagyu (pronounced "wah-gyoo") literally means "Japanese cow." This legendary beef is renowned worldwide for its exceptional marbling, buttery texture, and rich, umami flavor.

Superior Marbling

Intense intramuscular fat creates exceptional tenderness and that signature buttery texture Wagyu is famous for.

Healthier Fat Profile

Higher ratio of monounsaturated fats and omega-3s compared to conventional beef makes it a healthier choice.

Unmatched Flavor

Rich, buttery taste with deep umami notes that create a truly memorable dining experience.

Mountain Raised

Our Quality Promise

2.5 Year Raising Period

Each animal is raised for a full 2.5 years to ensure optimal marbling, flavor development, and tenderness—significantly longer than standard beef production.

Organic & Natural

No hormones, no antibiotics, no shortcuts. Just pure, natural raising practices that honor the land and the animals.

Family Operated

Personal attention to every animal, traditional raising practices, and a commitment to quality over quantity that only a family ranch can provide.
