Why We Raise Rocky Mountain Wagyu

At Rocky Mountain Wagyu, our mission is simple: To feed families healthy, organic Rocky Mountain Wagyu beef.

Meet one of our Rocky Mountain Wagyu calves—each animal at Rocky Mountain Wagyu receives individual care throughout their 2.5-year journey

We started Rocky Mountain Wagyu because we believe families deserve access to premium, healthy Rocky Mountain Wagyu beef raised with integrity. Every decision we make at Rocky Mountain Wagyu is guided by this simple principle: would we serve this Rocky Mountain Wagyu beef to our own families?

The answer is always yes. From our Utah mountain pastures to your table, Rocky Mountain Wagyu maintains the highest standards of animal welfare, environmental stewardship, and quality control. Our Rocky Mountain Wagyu cattle are raised without hormones or antibiotics, on natural mountain grazing lands in Utah that produce superior marbling and flavor in every Rocky Mountain steak.

Rocky Mountain Wagyu isn't just a business—it's our commitment to you and your family's health and happiness through premium Rocky Mountain Wagyu beef.

Rocky Mountain Wagyu Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about buying Rocky Mountain Wagyu beef and Rocky Mountain steaks from our Utah ranch

Where can I buy Rocky Mountain Wagyu beef?

You can buy Rocky Mountain Wagyu beef directly from Rocky Mountain Wagyu farm in Utah. We offer whole, half, and quarter Rocky Mountain Wagyu beef, plus individual Rocky Mountain steaks. Contact us at 801-301-3850 or rockymtnwagyu@gmail.com to order premium Rocky Mountain Wagyu.

What makes Rocky Mountain Wagyu different from regular beef?

Rocky Mountain Wagyu cattle are raised for 2.5 years in Utah's Rocky Mountains without hormones or antibiotics. Our Rocky Mountain Wagyu beef features superior marbling, healthier fat content, and exceptional flavor compared to conventional beef. Each Rocky Mountain steak is organic and mountain-raised on our Utah family ranch.

How long does it take to raise Rocky Mountain Wagyu?

Rocky Mountain Wagyu cattle are raised for 2.5 years from birth on our Utah ranch to ensure optimal marbling and flavor development in our Rocky Mountain steaks and beef. This is significantly longer than standard beef production, resulting in superior Rocky Mountain Wagyu quality.

Does Rocky Mountain Wagyu use hormones or antibiotics?

No. Rocky Mountain Wagyu is 100% organic. We never use hormones or antibiotics on our cattle at our Utah ranch. Our Rocky Mountain Wagyu beef and Rocky Mountain steaks are raised naturally in Utah's mountain pastures.

What Rocky Mountain Wagyu products do you offer?

Rocky Mountain Wagyu offers whole Rocky Mountain Wagyu beef, half beef, quarter beef, and individual Rocky Mountain steaks including ribeye, NY strip, and filet mignon. All our Rocky Mountain Wagyu products are organic and raised in Utah. Order Rocky Mountain Wagyu today from our family ranch!
