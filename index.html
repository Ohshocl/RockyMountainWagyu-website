Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about buying beef from our Utah ranch answered

Where can I buy Wagyu beef in Utah?

You can buy premium organic beef directly from our Utah ranch. We offer whole, half, and quarter options for families of all sizes. Contact us at 801-301-3850 or rockymtnwagyu@gmail.com to place your order today.

What makes your beef different from regular beef?

Our cattle are raised for 2.5 years in Utah's Rocky Mountains without hormones or antibiotics. The beef features superior marbling, healthier fat content, and exceptional flavor compared to conventional beef. All meat is organic and mountain-raised on our family ranch with traditional farming practices.

How long does it take to raise your cattle?

Our cattle are raised for a full 2.5 years from birth on our Utah ranch. This ensures optimal marbling and flavor development. This extended raising period is significantly longer than standard beef production, resulting in superior quality that's worth the wait.

Do you use hormones or antibiotics?

Absolutely not. We are 100% organic and committed to natural farming. We never use hormones or antibiotics on our cattle. The beef is raised naturally in Utah's pristine mountain pastures with traditional, ethical practices.

What products do you offer?

We offer whole, half, and quarter beef options to fit different family sizes and needs. All products are organic and raised in Utah's mountain pastures. Order today from our family ranch for farm-fresh delivery straight to your door!
