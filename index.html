About Rocky Mountain Wagyu

Rocky Mountain Wagyu is a family-operated ranch dedicated to raising premium Wagyu beef that exceeds industry standards. We take pride in our independent approach, focusing on quality that bridges American and Japanese Wagyu excellence.

Our cattle are raised in the beautiful Rocky Mountain region, where the natural environment and our careful raising practices create beef of exceptional quality. Each animal is raised for a full 2.5 years to ensure optimal marbling, flavor, and tenderness.

What Makes Us Unique:

  • Premium quality exceeding American Wagyu Association standards
  • Mountain-raised for superior flavor and texture
  • Family-operated with personal attention to every animal
  • Independent brand focused on excellence
  • 2.5-year raising period for optimal quality
