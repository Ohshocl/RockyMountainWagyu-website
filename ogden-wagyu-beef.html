Why Ogden Families Choose Our Wagyu Beef

We're a local Utah family farm committed to raising the finest beef in the Rocky Mountains. Our cattle enjoy 2.5 years of natural growth—far longer than industry standards—resulting in exceptional flavor and tenderness.

Raised in Utah

Born and raised right here in the Rocky Mountains, our cattle graze on pristine Utah land. Your beef travels less than 50 miles from our farm to your Ogden home.

100% Organic

No hormones, no antibiotics, no shortcuts. We raise our cattle the natural way, ensuring every cut of beef meets the highest organic standards your family expects.

2.5 Year Timeline

Unlike commercial operations that rush cattle to market, we allow our Wagyu a full 2.5 years to mature naturally. This extended timeline creates superior marbling and unforgettable flavor.

Family Farm Values

We're not a corporation—we're a Utah family dedicated to quality over quantity. When you order from us, you're supporting local agriculture and getting beef we're proud to serve our own families.

Three Butcher Options

Choose what works best for you: pick up from our farm and use your own butcher, have us deliver to your chosen butcher, or use our butcher service where we coordinate everything for you.

Transparency First

Know exactly where your beef comes from. We believe in complete transparency about our raising practices, timeline, and commitment to organic standards.

What Makes Our Wagyu Special

Wagyu beef is renowned worldwide for its incredible marbling, tenderness, and rich flavor. Our Rocky Mountain location provides the perfect environment for raising exceptional cattle.

We believe in doing things differently. While most beef operations focus on speed and volume, we focus on quality and care. Our extended 2.5-year raising period allows for natural development and superior marbling that you can see and taste in every bite.

  • American Wagyu genetics for superior marbling
  • Grass-fed and grain-finished for optimal flavor
  • Stress-free environment in Utah's mountains
  • No growth hormones or unnecessary antibiotics
  • Locally processed by trusted Utah butchers
  • Available in whole, half, or quarter portions

Easy Ordering for Ogden Residents

Getting premium Wagyu beef for your family is simple. We offer whole, half, and quarter beef options to fit your needs.

You have three butcher options: pick up from our farm and take to your own butcher, have us deliver to your chosen butcher, or use our butcher service where we coordinate everything. Each order includes a variety of premium cuts—steaks, roasts, ground beef, and more.

  • Choose your portion size (whole, half, or quarter)
  • Select from three butcher service options
  • Specify your cutting instructions and preferences
  • Choose vacuum sealed or butcher paper packaging
  • Flexible pickup arrangements
  • Personal service from our family to yours
