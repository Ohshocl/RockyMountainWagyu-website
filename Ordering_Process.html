How to Order

Our ordering process is straightforward and personal. We'll work with you every step of the way to ensure you get exactly what you need.

1

Contact Us

Give us a call at 801-301-3850 or email us at rockymtnwagyu@gmail.com to discuss your needs. We'll help you determine the right option for your family—whether that's a whole beef, half, or quarter.

We operate on a farming timeline, so don't worry about rushing. We're happy to take the time to answer all your questions and explain the process in detail, including pricing and butcher options.

2

Select Your Beef

Choose from our available cattle or reserve a spot in our pre-sale program. Each animal is raised for a full 2.5 years to ensure optimal marbling and flavor.

We sell whole, half, and quarter beef only. This ensures you get the best value and complete control over how your beef is cut and packaged.

3

Choose Your Butcher Option

You have three options for processing your beef:

Standard: Use Your Own Butcher

  • Pick up your beef from our ranch
  • Take it to your preferred butcher
  • Work directly with them on cutting instructions
  • Most economical option

Premium: Delivery to Your Butcher

  • We deliver to your chosen butcher
  • You specify cutting instructions with your butcher
  • Saves you the transport hassle
  • Additional delivery fee based on distance

Premium: Use Our Butcher

  • We coordinate everything with our trusted local butcher
  • You choose your cuts and package sizes
  • Choice of vacuum sealed or butcher paper packaging
  • Additional processing fee (discussed when ordering)
4

Specify Your Cuts

If you're using your own butcher or having us deliver to your butcher, you'll work directly with them to specify how you want your beef cut and packaged.

If you're using our butcher service, we'll provide you with a cutting instructions form. Choose your preferred steak thickness, roast sizes, and ground beef package amounts. We're happy to provide recommendations based on your family's cooking preferences if this is your first time.

5

Pickup Your Beef

Standard Option: Pick up your live animal or hanging carcass from our ranch and transport to your butcher.

Using Our Butcher: We'll contact you when your beef is processed, packaged, and ready for pickup. We'll coordinate a convenient time that works with your schedule.

Make sure you have adequate freezer space ready—we recommend 16-20 cubic feet for a whole beef, 8-10 cubic feet for a half, and 4-5 cubic feet for a quarter.

Our Butcher Service Options

If you choose to use our butcher service (premium option), you can select your packaging method. Both options preserve quality and freshness.

Vacuum Sealed

Our vacuum-sealed packaging removes all air from the package, providing maximum protection against freezer burn and extending shelf life up to 2-3 years in the freezer.

Benefits: Longest freezer life, prevents freezer burn, easy to see contents, space-efficient stacking

*Available only when using our butcher service

Butcher Paper

Traditional butcher paper wrapping provides excellent protection while being more eco-friendly. Beef stays fresh for 6-12 months in the freezer when properly wrapped.

Benefits: Traditional method, environmentally friendly, easy to label, classic presentation

*Available only when using our butcher service

Pricing Information

Butcher service pricing varies based on the size of your order and packaging preferences. We'll provide a detailed cost breakdown when you place your order.

Contact us to discuss: Current processing fees, delivery charges (if applicable), and estimated total costs for your specific order.

What to Expect

Here's a general timeline for the ordering process from start to finish.

📞

Initial Contact

Reach out to us and we'll discuss your needs, answer questions, and help you select the right option—whole, half, or quarter beef. We'll also explain butcher options and pricing.

🐄

Selection & Reservation

Choose from currently available beef or reserve a spot in our pre-sale program. We'll provide all the details about timing and expectations.

🔪

Butcher Coordination

Depending on your choice: coordinate with your own butcher, or we'll handle everything with our butcher service. If using our butcher, you'll receive cutting instructions to specify your preferences.

📦

Processing (If Using Our Butcher: 2-3 weeks)

Your beef is professionally butchered and aged to perfection, then cut and packaged according to your specifications.

✅

Pickup

Standard option: Pick up from our ranch and transport to your butcher. Premium option: We'll notify you when your processed beef is ready for pickup. Bring your coolers and get ready to stock your freezer with premium Wagyu!
